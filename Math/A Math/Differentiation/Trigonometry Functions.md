# Sine

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\sin^b{f(x)} = b \cdot f'(x) \cdot \cos{f(x)} \cdot \sin^{b-1}{f(x)}$$

# Cosine

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\cos^b{f(x)} = b \cdot f'(x) \cdot -\sin{f(x)} \cdot \cos^{b-1}{f(x)} $$

# Tangent

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\tan^b{f(x)} = b \cdot f'(x) \cdot \sec^2{f(x)} \cdot tan^{b-1}{f(x)} $$
