# Sine

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\sin^n(ax+b) = n \cdot a \cdot \cos(ax+b) \cdot \sin^{b-1}(ax+b)$$

# Cosine

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\cos^n(ax+b) = n \cdot a \cdot -\sin(ax+b) \cdot \cos^{b-1}(ax+b)$$

# Tangent

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\tan^{n}(ax+b) = n \cdot a \cdot \sec^{2}(ax+b) \cdot tan^{b-1}(ax+b) $$
