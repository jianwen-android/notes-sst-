# Natural logarithm

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\ln f(x) = \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)} $$

# Logarithm with real base

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\log_af(x) = \frac{d}{dx}\frac{ln(f(x))}{ln(a)} = \frac{f'(x)}{\ln (a)*f(x)} $$
