# Rules

##  Power Rule

$$ \frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1} $$

Where n is a real number

## Chain Rule

$$ \frac{d}{dx} (ax^b + 5)^c = c\times(ax^b + 5)^{c-1}\times abx $$

Where a, b and c are real numbers

## Product Rule

$$ \frac{d}{dx}f(x)g(x) = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x) $$

## Quotient Rule
